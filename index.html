Hilltop Memories

Event Center

Hilltop Memories is a spacious venue conveniently located between Austin and Waco, away from the hustle and bustle of the city. The scenery is unmatched, sitting atop a beautiful hill, overlooking a quiet valley with lots of wildlife, and a great view of the sunset. We are set up to host a variety of celebrations from weddings and quinceaneras to baby showers and graduations.

What We Offer

1

An accommodating space

Between our indoor venue and the land we built it on, our space is perfect for both scenic outdoor events and extravagant indoor gatherings. We also have a great setup for your band or DJ, a kitchen and serving area, separate bridal/groom rooms, and private entries for the bride, band, caterers, etc.

2

A safe space

Our indoor/outdoor facilities are safe for all ages, with plenty of space for the kids to play and various areas for the adults to convene. The property is completely fenced in and set back from a country road leaving lots of room for running, or for an outdoor setup.

3

A private space

The country-side location of our venue means that your party is separated from outside distractions, giving you complete privacy to enjoy the celebration with your friends and family without having to think about the rest of the world.

What's Unique About Us

A Personalized Setup

Included in our rental fees is the use of our equipment, such as tables, chairs, serving tables, beverage/food insulator and all of our kitchen appliances. We also offer use of our props for photography shoots, or table decor.

A Tech-Forward Finish

Inside the venue, there are two 75” TV’s on either side of the stage, perfect for slideshow memories, or watching the game. In the bridal sweet we have Hollywood mirrors offering three light modes, bluetooth speakers, and wireless charging, and in the groom’s room you’ll find another flat screen TV. On top of that, high speed internet is available throughout the venue.

A Truly Texas View

The outside of our venue offers a perfect view of Texas over the hilltop, making a beautiful backdrop for your event. Besides the valley, there is a nice view of our rustic barn decorated with antique tools from the 1900’s, and miles of country roads surrounded by tall, shady trees.
